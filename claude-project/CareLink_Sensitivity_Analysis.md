# Part 7: Key Insights Summary

## 7.1 What We Learned

### The Business is NOT Sensitive To:
- Small variations in fixed costs (±20% = ±$150K impact)
- Payment processing fee changes
- Office/admin expenses
- Exact marketing channel mix (if total CAC stays constant)

### The Business IS Highly Sensitive To:
1. **Churn rate** (±10% churn = ±$1.2M Y5 impact)
2. **ARPA** (±20% ARPA = ±$890K Y5 impact)
3. **Customer volume** (±25% volume = ±$720K Y5 impact)

### The Business Breaks When:
- Churn exceeds 35% (LTV falls below 3x CAC)
- ARPA falls below $4,000 (never reaches profitability)
- CAC exceeds $1,800 (payback exceeds 12 months)
- Volume reaches only 50% of target (cash exhaustion Y2)

## 7.3 The Final Answer

> **CareLink AI is not "profitable" or "unprofitable."**
